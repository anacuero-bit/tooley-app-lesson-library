Why "Tooley"?

Named after the researcher who discovered something remarkable.

In the slums of Hyderabad, Lagos, and Nairobi, Professor James Tooley found something that surprised the education establishment: poor parents were paying for private schools out of their own pockets, even when free government schools existed nearby.

Why? Because these small, scrappy, community-run schools actually taught their children. Teachers‚Äîoften without formal degrees‚Äîwere delivering results that put well-funded government schools to shame.

"Wherever I went, I found private schools in the slums... The teachers in these schools were dedicated, and they were getting results."
